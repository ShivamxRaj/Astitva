Avyakta - Unidentified Deceased & Missing Persons Portal

Avyakta is India's humanitarian platform connecting families with unclaimed and unidentified deceased persons since 2020. Our mission is to restore dignity to every unclaimed body and bring closure to grieving families through officer coordination, anonymous reporting, and community support.

Report a Case

Report details about an unclaimed or unidentified deceased person. You can choose to submit the report anonymously. Our officers coordinate with hospitals, police stations, and volunteers across India to identify and resolve cases.

The Reality We Face

Every year, thousands of unidentified bodies go unclaimed across India. Many families spend years searching for their missing loved ones without support. Avyakta bridges this gap through humanitarian case coordination, connecting officers, hospitals, and families.

Why Choose Avyakta

Anonymous reporting for safety. Officer-coordinated case handling. Pan-India coverage across Punjab, Delhi, Mumbai, and more. AI-powered facial recognition for matching. Dignity-first approach to identification.
